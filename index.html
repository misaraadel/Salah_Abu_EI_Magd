About

See more about Salah Abu-Elmaged

Salah Abu EI-Magd is an entrepreneur with more than 15 years of experience in teaching, mindset building and training. He also has 2 masters in international business law and economics from Ain Shams University and IU Mckinney .

His journey as an entrepreneur is continuing to be the CEO of ACTA for training & consultancy and founder of TMS the digital marketing & advertising agency for handling and helping a great bunch of multiple clients in the current market with the needed digital activities .

As an Executive Coach, Salah participated in training and motivational speeches to more than 50,000 attendees with diversified age groups, cultural, and professional backgrounds as well .

Moreover, He conducted corporate training and development for big organizations like Careem, Zain Telecom, Philip morris, Taqa Gas, and Takaful Misr in order to help professionals perform better, communicate better, sell better.
